What is an AI agent?

An AI agent is software that can pursue a goal step by step — using your tools, data, and rules — instead of only chatting. Think of it as a digital worker that can complete tasks, not just answer questions.

Do I need to understand AI to work with you?

No. Just tell us the business problem. We handle the AI design, tools, and setup — and explain what you’ll get in plain language.

What kinds of problems can AI solve?

Everyday business busywork — the same things people do over and over. For example:

  • An automatic email reply agent for common customer questions
  • A website or chat answering machine that works 24/7
  • Filling spreadsheets and reports for you instead of typing by hand
  • Following up with leads and appointment reminders automatically
  • Booking or rescheduling meetings without phone tag
  • Sending order / status updates so your team doesn’t repeat itself
  • Sorting inbox messages and sending them to the right person

If it’s repetitive and rules-based, AI is often a strong fit. Tell us your problem in plain words — we’ll suggest what to build.
